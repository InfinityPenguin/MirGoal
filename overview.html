Overview

MirGoal is a clinical-application biotechnology company developing a broad pipeline of leading microRNA-based oncology diagnostics, the first to be tested in cancer patients.

Company

MirGoal was newly founded in 2015 to take the mission to advance the micro RNA technologies for its applications in medical diagnostics. We are committed to providing professional, state-of-the-art research and development laboratory services to pharmaceutical industry, clinical laboratories, members of molecular diagnostic and clinical communities, and academic researchers.

Mission

We strive to be recognized as the preferred provider of research and development services that lead to new laboratory testing and professional interpretative services in laboratory diagnostics specialized in MicroRNAs. We strive to become a global leader in microRNA laboratory science from U.S. to solve difficult cases beyond the competency of ordinary clinical reference laboratories. Just as important to us, we seek to make real contributions to our community through the excellence of our services, professional interactions with our clients, and the education of future leaders in the areas of microRNA. Developed under the direction of microRNA experts renowned throughout the academic and medical fields, MirGoal intends to develop a portfolio of a unique menu of cancer diagnostic and treatment tests—all readily available to clients ranging from hospitals and universities, to reference laboratories. We provide expertise, service and innovation that cannot find anywhere else.

Core Competency

Our core competency is to academic and medical research on miRNAs, and the ability to establish all components required offering a robust clinical laboratory test for routine operation in reference laboratories, and at the point of care. Our in-depth understanding of the need of contract research from pharmaceutical companies is also an important component of the companies' asset. MirGoal is currently composed of 5 highly trained and well experienced professionals. We have a staff dedicated to clinical and contracted research businesses including: Pathologist Dr. Huiqing Wu, statisticians and medical researcher Dr. Xiwei Wu, Dr. Ju Jingfang, a pioneer academic researcher with leadership in translational medicine in microRNA research field, and Dr. Dongling Ma, a PhD. senior scientist that conducts hands-on experiments, and design all operational details for a routine clinical laboratory test. President and CEO Dr. Wenzhe Li is a PhD certified clinical chemist, well trained in academic settings as well as clinical laboratory and industrial settings. Experienced in management of R&D and business development, she will lead the team to execute the commercialization plan. Mr. Pierre Cassigneul, CEO of NMS LABS, is a board of director for MirGoal, who will continue to help us leverage our legacy relationship with NMS LABS via contracted research services and continued access to clinical laboratory data to fuel continued development and success of commercialization of our microRNA biomarkers pipelines.

Value Proposition for Customers

  1. Our services improve their access to new molecular diagnostic tests, data and insights compiled through scientific collaborations. We provide a centralized resource for the industry including reference labs, hospitals, and large practice groups.
  2. Our new product provides pharmaceutical researchers with a new stream of tools of microRNA diagnostic and prognostic insights that can lead to better therapy selection and access.
  3. We deliver economic focus for colorectal cancer patients and healthcare system, help patients improve their quality of life; eliminate wasteful expenditures on therapies that do not work.
